Coffee Boba Tea(Dalgona)

Coffee boba tea is sweet and creamy, with a subtle coffee bitterness balanced by the sweet tapioca pearls. It has a smooth and thick texture, transferring you to another world instantly.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. instant coffee powder
  • 2 tsp. brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp. water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp. cooked boba pearls of choice
  • Ice cubes(optional!)

Instructions

  1. Cooking the boba pearls(instructions for tapioca only):

    Bring 8 times the water mentioned above to a boil, then add the tapioca pearls. Read the instructions on the back of the package carefully, and find the required time, since it may be anywhere from 5 - 20 minutes. Cover the lid, turn off the stove, and let sit until cool.

  2. Whipping your coffee:

    Add 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder, 2 teaspoons of brown sugar, then soon after, add around 2 tablespoons of hot, boiling water. Using a milk frother, whip the entire mixture for a minute or so. Then scrape the sides and whip any lumps that didn't get whipped.

  3. Assemble:

    Add the boba pearls first and then your favorite milk. You can try soy milk, almond milk, oat milk, or regular milk. Optionally, add some ice cubes and then add the whipped coffee on top

Tips:

  • To make it taste more bitter, add more coffee. For a sweeter taste, add more brown sugar or milk.
