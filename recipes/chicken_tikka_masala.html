Chicken Tikka Masala

Simple, easy, and absolutely one of the most delicious spins on chicken curry! This recipe consists of tender, char-grilled chicken, simmered in a splendid, aromatic sauce.

Ingredients

For marinade:

  • 28 oz of boneless skinless chick thighs cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 ½ tbsp garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger fresh crushed or minced
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri chili or ½ teaspoon ground red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 small onions or 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic finely grated
  • 1 tablespoon ginger finely grated
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 14 ounces tomato puree tomato sauce/Passata
  • 1 teaspoon Kashmiri chili optional for color and flavor
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder adjust to your taste preference
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups heavy or thickened cream, use evaporated milk for lower calories
  • ¼ cup water if needed
  • 4 tablespoons fresh cilantro/coriander to garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl combine chicken with all ingredients for the chicken sauce then let marinate for 10 to an hour depending on your schedule.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat then once sizzling add chicken pieces in batches of two or three. Make sure not to crowd the pan. Fry till brown only 3 minutes on each side.
  3. Melt butter in the pan then fry onions until they are soft while scraping any leftover bits stuck on the bottom of the pan
  4. Add garlic and ginger and saute for 1 minute until the scent starts wafting around the room then add garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and coriander. Fry again for about 20 seconds until fragrant and stir occasionally.
  5. Then, pour in tomato puree, chili powder, and salt and let it simmer for about 10 to 15 minutes stirring occasionally until the sauce thickens and becomes a deep brown color.
  6. Stir cream through the sauce add chicken and sauce into the pan and cook for a few more minutes.

Tips:

  • For extra smokiness, grill the chicken before adding it to the sauce
  • Serve with basmati rice and garlic naan bread for the best experience
