Napolitan Pasta

Need a quick and effortless family dinner that takes 20 minutes or less to cook? Look no further, and embrace this easy, fancy, and amazing recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp non-dairy milk
  • 1 tbsp Japanese Worcester sauce
  • Tbsp of soy sauce

For pasta (for 2 servings):

  • Spaghetti noodles - 200 grams
  • Garlic cloves - minced
  • 1 onion - thinly sliced
  • 6-8 button mushrooms - thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper - thinly sliced
  • 2 vegan sausages

Instructions

  1. First, boil the noodles at their appropriate time.
  2. While boiling, fry the garlic and onions with oil, or use the healthier option, and go oil-free. Once the veggies turn translucent, add mushrooms and bell pepper and saute for 2 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium on the stove, move vegetables to the side of the frying pan, Then add the sausages and fry them for about 1 minute on each side until you get a little browning on all sides.
  4. Then add all the sauce ingredients and mix the pasta and toss everything using tongs.
  5. Season with salt and pepper if you prefer. Then serve with tabasco sauce, vegan parmesan cheese, or parsley.

Tips:

  • Top with a fried egg for an interesting new flavor
  • For a more authentic taste, buy ketchup from Japanese grocery stores or use Kagome Tomato Ketchup
