Chicken Fried Rice

A savory mix of fluffy, slightly crispy rice with a distinct chicken flavor, enhanced by the addition of eggs, vegetables like carrots and peas, and a blend of Asian seasonings like soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sometimes a hint of sesame oil: this, my friend, is the perfect chicken fried rice, or you could say, the World Foodie chicken fried rice.

Ingredients

  • White/brown rice(either one works)
  • Diced chicken breast
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Fresh or frozen carrots and peas(Note that you will have to cook the fresh vegetables longer)
  • Green onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a skillet or wok, heat up 1 ½ tsp of vegetable oil and another 1 ½ tsp of sesame oil over medium to high heat.
  2. Add in your diced chicken pieces, and season them a little bit with salt and pepper, and cook it for about 5-6 minutes.
  3. Set aside your chicken and get ready to move on.
  4. Make sure your skillet or wok is at medium high heat again, and add another 1 ½ tsp sesame oil and 1 ½ tsp vegetable oil.
  5. Add in your carrots and peas, and start to saute for about a minute. Then, add in your garlic, and saute for another minute(fresh vegetables take a minute longer).
  6. Start to push your veggies to the end of the pan.
  7. Once all veggies are lining the outside of your pan add the eggs in the middle and scramble them until set.
  8. Add chicken back in with your rice as well, and then add in soy sauce to taste, then season with salt and pepper.
  9. Toss everything together and enjoy it hot or warm.

Tips:

  • Fluff your cooked rice for no mushy or clumpy rice
  • Season the rice and chicken for a more combustive flavour
