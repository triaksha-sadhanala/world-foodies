Taro Boba Tea

The onions and masala mix in your mouth, creating a tapestry of delicious flavors, especially with the crunchy layer texture of fried dough.

Ingredients

Boba pearls:

  • 1/4 cup uncooked boba pearls
  • water for boiling the boba pearls
  • 1/4 cup. brown sugar

Assembling:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp. taro paste

Instructions

  1. Bring the amount of water needed to cook your boba pearls and bring it to a boil. Drop the boba pearls in and let them boil. Take them out and let them rest according to the package.
  2. Rinse the pearls under water then mix your pearls with sugar syrup or white sugar
  3. Scoop about 3 tablespoons of boba pearls into your cup and scoop taro paste onto the inside of the cup
  4. Add in ice cubes and pour in some milk make sure to stir well before serving
