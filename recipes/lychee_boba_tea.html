Lychee Boba Tea

Lychee boba tea has a sweet, tropical fruit flavor with a floral aroma and a confounding taste when you bite into the tapioca pearls.

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh lychee peeled and seeds removed
  • 1 cup milk
  • Cooked boba pearls
  • 1 tbsp. jasmine green tea
  • 1 cup hot water around 80 degrees Celsius
  • 1 tbsp. syrup

Instructions

  1. Cook the boba pearls:

    Bring 8 times of water to a boil and then add tapioca pearls. Read the instructions on the package (find the required time). It may be 5 to 10 min. Cover the lid, turn off the stove, then let sit until cool. Rinse the boba pearls under running water. Then mix the boba pearls with brown sugar or syrup.

  2. Steep jasmine green tea:

    Steep jasmine green tea for around 5 minutes. Remove the seeds and set aside to cool down.Make lychee tea base, then peel lychee and remove the black seed. Transfer the flesh into a shaker and smash the fresh flesh until juicy and pureed. Then add cooled jasmine green tea base and syrup to taste. Gently shake for a while and avoid over-shaking. The lychee jasmine green tea base is ready.

  3. Assemble:

    Add boba pearls, and ice cubes if you are doing an iced version, then pour in the milk of choice. Top with lychee tea base.

Tips:

  • If you want the flavour to infuse, just let the tea sit in the fridge for an hour.
