Mango Boba Tea

Mango boba tea has a refreshing and tropical taste that is perfect for the summer and hot seasons.

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh mango, pitted
  • 5 ice cubes
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup cooked boba pearls

Instructions

  1. First, pour all the ingredients except the tapioca pearls and blend until smooth.
  2. Make the tapioca pearls on the stove using medium heat.
  3. Once all the pearls are cooked and the blend is all smooth, grab a mason jar or cup and place the tapioca pearls, then pour in the blend.
  4. Enjoy!

Tips:

  • For a more refreshing taste, add mango ice cream or replace the milk with vanilla ice cream and a little extra mango.
  • Use fresh mango puree for a vibrant flavor.
  • Consider a lighter tea like green tea to avoid bitterness.
  • Adjust sweetness with simple syrup to your preference.
  • Add a touch of acidity like lemon juice for balance.
  • Experiment with toppings such as fresh mango chunks, ice cubes, or honey for a sense of extra dimension.
